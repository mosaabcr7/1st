Biography of Karim Benzema

Karim Hafez Mustafa Benzema is a French footballer who plays in the attacking position with Real Madrid in the Spanish League and the France national team. Benzema is known for his aerial abilities, high work rate, playmaking and good finishing. Benzema is considered one of the best strikers of his generation. Benzema was born in Lyon to parents of Algerian descent from Oran.
